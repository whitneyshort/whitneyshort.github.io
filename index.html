About Me

I am finishing up my first year of my doctoral program in Chemistry at the University of Chicago. Alongside my interests in quantum chemistry, I have a passion for mentorship and teaching.

With an undergraduate degree in both Chemistry and English Literature, I have learned the power of an interdisciplinary education firsthand. This synthesis of "disparate" fields of study has equipped me with strong science communication skills, creativity, and a soft spot for factitive verbs.

My hobbies include going to the movies, vintage shopping, decorating my apartment and interior design, caring for my cats and fishkeeping, and pursuit of an iced triple latte.
