College Essay Editing & Guidance

Throughout my degree in English Literature, I took several advanced grammar and style courses which have given me the skills to aid in college essay refinement at the clause, sentence, and paragraph level. I can help you hone your own personal voice and also provide guidance in content and structure to help convey as much as possible about you to the reader in the limited word and character counts allotted.

I work with high school students on the Common App personal statement, supplemental essays, and scholarship essays. Especially in the era of AI-aided writing, I will help you craft your individual voice and style, without sacrificing the important task of communicating who you are and strive to become. College essays are a unique genre of writing where a little cheesiness can be more than welcome. I believe in employing playfulness, creative sentence structure, and literary devices within my writing, without sacrificing the rigor or depth of the content of the essay. For supplemental essays, we will include specific details about schools to show you did your homework, tailored to the story you're telling across all your materials. We will ensure that we use each component to reinforce broader themes, while still introducing new pieces of information about yourself to tell the admissions committee a coherent, strong story.
